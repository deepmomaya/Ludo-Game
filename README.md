# Ludo

A classic implementation of **Ludo** — one of the world's most popular board games — built in Java with a clean graphical interface.

Roll the dice, move your pieces around the board, capture your opponents, and race all four tokens home to win. Simple to pick up, endlessly fun to play.

## Features

- 4-player local multiplayer
- Animated dice rolls and piece movement
- Capture mechanics with extra turn rewards
- Safe squares and home lane logic
- Clean, colorful board UI

## How to Play

Each player takes turns rolling the dice. Roll a **6** to bring a piece out of the yard. Move your pieces clockwise around the board and into the home lane. The first player to get all four pieces home wins.
